## k8s_training – Vibe-Engineered Homelab & Exam Track

This repo pulls together a **vibe‑engineered** Kubernetes learning path built around your homelab and the `k8s_training` directory.

- **Homelab & JobMatchAI modules (`k8s_training/jobmatch_ai_docs`)**: Zero‑to‑practical series that walks you from homelab foundations and k3s install, through Kubernetes core primitives, Kustomize, and FluxCD GitOps, all the way to running and operating the `jobmatch_ai` app (plus ingress, TLS/DNS, observability, backups, and advanced homelab topics).
- **Exam prep track (`k8s_training/exam_prep`)**: Focused Kubernetes certification prep layered on top of the homelab work, including exam overview, picking CKA/CKAD and building a study plan, topic checklists mapped to your modules, hands‑on practice labs inside your k3s cluster, and a full practice‑exam scenario you can rerun.

Everything is intentionally **vibe engineered**: the docs assume a real homelab, practical workflows, and an opinionated but flexible path that’s meant to feel motivating, not like dry certification boilerplate.
