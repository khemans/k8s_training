## Kubernetes Exam Prep – Overview

**Goal:** Build on your homelab training to prepare for your first Kubernetes certification (e.g. CKA/CKAD) using focused practice and a realistic exam-like environment.

This track is separate from the **zero-to-master homelab modules** and assumes you’ve completed (or almost completed) those modules.

---

### Recommended Path

1. **Finish core homelab modules**
   - At minimum: Modules 0–4 (cluster, core resources, Kustomize, Flux) and preferably through Module 6 (`jobmatch_ai` running on k3s).
2. **Pick a target exam**
   - Common first options:
     - CKA – Certified Kubernetes Administrator.
     - CKAD – Certified Kubernetes Application Developer.
3. **Use exam-prep modules**
   - `EP_01_Exam_Selection_And_Plan.md` – choose exam + study plan.
   - `EP_02_Core_Topics_Checklist.md` – map your skills to exam objectives.
   - `EP_03_Practice_Labs_In_Cluster.md` – timed, task-based labs running in your k3s cluster.
   - `EP_04_Practice_Exam_Scenario.md` – full-length practice exam you can rerun.

Over time, you can iterate on these to track readiness and simulate the exam more closely.
